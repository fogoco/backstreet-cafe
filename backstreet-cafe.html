Our Story

More than a coffee.
A place to belong.

Since 2016, Backstreet Cafe has been the Sunshine Coast's best-kept secret — tucked away steps from Mooloolaba Beach. We're not just another café. We're a community gathering spot where quality is never compromised and every guest leaves feeling at home.

From our meticulously sourced beans to our seasonal menu built around local producers, every detail is intentional. Because we believe that great coffee and great food should be everyday luxuries.
